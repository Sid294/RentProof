Features

Everything you need to run a professional operation.

Rent tracking

Payment status at a glance

See every unit's payment status in real time. Paid, late, partial -- all on one screen. No calls, no spreadsheet updates, no guessing.

Move-in documentation

Timestamped, locked, permanent

Tenants complete a guided photo walkthrough on day one. Every image is timestamped and locked -- it cannot be edited or deleted by anyone.

Maintenance management

Submitted, tracked, resolved

Tenants submit maintenance requests with photos directly in the app. You assign, update, and close them. Every action is logged with a timestamp.

Deposit vault

Protected from the day they move in

Track deposit amounts and legal return deadlines for every unit. One click generates a complete dispute evidence package if it ever goes to court.

Lease and document storage

Every document in one place

Upload leases, notices, addendums, and signed agreements. Both parties always have access. Nothing gets lost in an email thread.

In-app messaging

Every conversation on record

Every landlord-tenant exchange is timestamped, logged, and stored. No more lost texts. No more "you never told me." The record speaks for itself.
